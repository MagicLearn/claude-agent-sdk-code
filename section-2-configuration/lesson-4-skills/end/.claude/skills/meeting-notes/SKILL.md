---
name: meeting-notes
description: Creates structured meeting notes from raw notes or transcripts. Use when asked to organize meeting notes, summarize a meeting, or create action items from a transcript.
---

When creating meeting notes:

1. Start with a header: date, attendees, and topic
2. Write a 2-3 sentence summary of the meeting
3. List key decisions made
4. List action items with owners and deadlines
5. Note any open questions or follow-ups

Format the output as markdown. Keep it concise — no one reads long meeting notes.
